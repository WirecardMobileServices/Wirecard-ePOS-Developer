@discussion sendCollectedSignature - callback to Extension once the Payment Flow signalls the Approval/Rejection of the signature It is used on the client side to confirm the signature validity If nil then extension awaits the confirmation of the signature - e.g. by pressing the terminal buttons If NOT nil then terminal does not have buttons and confirmation must be handled by the client application (presenting the OK/Cancel buttons to confirm the signature validity)
