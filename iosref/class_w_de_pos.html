Detailed Description

Wirecard Payment Acceptance SDK.

Enables your solution to Accept a wide range of Payment Methods (Cash, Card, Alipay, WeChat, Coupon) and offers advanced functionality to support your business

Sale Management - WDSaleManager - Sales, Payments, Refunds, Sales history, Sale Statistics, Receipts

Cash Management - WDCashManager - Cash flow, Shifts, Cash Registers, Z-Reports

Device Management - WDTerminalManager, WDPrinterManager, WDCashDrawerManager, WDScannerManager - Devices support

Inventory Management - WDProductCatalogueManager - Product Catalogues, Prices, Stocks, SAP ByDesign integration

User Management - WDUserManager - Current User, Credential management

Customer Management - WDCustomerManager - Members information
