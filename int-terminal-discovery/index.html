    Terminal Discovery

    The discoverDevices method of Terminal Manager handles discovery of all paired and connected Posmate terminals. Spire manufactures the following terminals:

    • Posmate
    • SPm2
    • Spm20

    All three of these terminals are served by the PosMateExtension.
